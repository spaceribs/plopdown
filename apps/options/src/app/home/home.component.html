Add a Plopdown Track

Upload, paste a URL, or make one yourself

Plopdown Tracks are special video subtitles with data instead of text. They allow precise, custom, and well formed annotations with simple/readable JSON encoding.

Find a Matching Video

Any hosting website will do!

Use the plopdown extension button to scan the current page for videos to attach tracks to. Once you associate a video to a track, clicking the extension button will automatically initialize your track.

Setup Autoplay

No button pressing needed!

If you provide us permission, we can immediately initialize your track whenever you visit those videos.

Write your own Tracks

Use our built in devtools to annotate videos!

No more needless raw editing of text files, now you can add/edit annotations directly in your browser.
